**Patent Number:** US 12,345,678

**Abstract:** This patent describes systems and methods for automated processing of insurance applications using machine learning models. The invention covers the integration of multiple data sources, risk scoring algorithms, and decision support mechanisms.

**Claims:** Methods for multi-modal data fusion, risk classification, and confidence-based routing to human review.
